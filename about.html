About Meridian Arch Studio

Who We Are

At Meridian Arch Studio, we are passionate about shaping spaces that reflect both innovation and tradition. Based in Kerala, our team of visionary architects and designers crafts residential, commercial, and cultural environments that harmonize with the region’s rich heritage while embracing modern aesthetics.

With over a decade of experience, we specialize in sustainable architecture, smart planning, and client-centered design. Each project is a collaboration — a journey where creativity meets functionality to deliver unique, timeless structures.
